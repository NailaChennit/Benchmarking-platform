What is Benchmarking ?

Derived from the word "benchmark" which means reference or benchmark, benchmarking is the comparison of performance or processes with other entities, including competitors that represent competitive companies in the same domain.

Benchmarking has been a popular practice in the business world since the 1990s. This comparative analysis aims to "copy" or imitate the techniques (services or products) of competitors, but not just any competitors. is about finding what we can describe as "good competitor", and this among a very large number of references. A good competitor is a company with characteristics, also called KPIs, close to our company but which is more efficient. To find this good competing experts manually analyze a huge volume of business data.
